Stainless Steel 201 Sheet and Plates

ASTM A240 / UNS S20100 / 1.4372 Sheet and Plates

SS 201 is an austenitic type manufactured from a combination of elements like chromium, nickel, and manganese. SS 201 Plates have excellent properties such as high tensile strength, resistance to high temperatures and pressure, robust construction, and so on. Stainless steel grades 201 plates are easy to shape and corrosion-resistant. Stainless Steel 201 sheets are widely used in various industries and applications due to their excellent corrosion resistance, durability, and formability. In the construction industry, they are often used for roofing, siding, and structural components. They are also commonly used in the automotive and transportation industries for exhaust systems, trim, and other components. In the food and beverage industry, they are used for food processing equipment, kitchen appliances, and storage tanks.

What are the useful features of stainless steel grades 201 plate?

Stainless steel grades 201 plates are highly durable, corrosion resistant and have excellent weldability.
