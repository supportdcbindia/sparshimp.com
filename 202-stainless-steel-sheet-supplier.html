What is stainless steel 202 sheet hs code?
The HS code for the Stainless Steel 202 Sheet is 7219 3300. It can be classified under flat-rolled stainless steel products with a thickness of not less than 4. 75 mm or more.

What are the common finishes for Stainless Steel 202 Sheet?
Some of the most preferred Stainless Steel 202 Sheet finishes are 2B (polished, shiny, reflective), No. 1 (rough, matte, produced by hot rolling), and BA (polished, shiny, mirror-like). These finishes have different appearance and anti-corrosive properties. It depends on the specific application that you require.

Is Stainless Steel 202 Sheet magnetic?
SS202 Sheet is slightly magnetic since it contains manganese in large proportion and nickel in small proportion. It possesses a little magnetism, particularly in cold working, but is not as magnetic as other stainless steel types.
