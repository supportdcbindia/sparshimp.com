Can Stainless Steel 304l Channel Be Welded?
Yes, the stainless steel 304L channel can be welded through TIG welding, MIG welding, and arc welding. It has low carbon content, reducing problems such as carbide precipitation and therefore, it can be welded without extensive post weld heat treatment.
