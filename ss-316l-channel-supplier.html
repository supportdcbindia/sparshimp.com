Can Stainless Steel 316l Channel Be Welded?
Yes, Stainless Steel 316L channel can be welded using conventional welding techniques such as TIG, MIG and arc welding. It contains a low percentage of carbon, thus minimising the possibility of carbide formation; hence, it can be welded without the need for complex heat treatment processes after welding.

How Should SS 316l Channel Be Cleaned and Maintained?
For cleaning and maintaining SS 316L channel, one should use mild soap and water accompanied by a cloth or sponge. Do not use abrasive cleaners and chemicals on the surfaces as they can cause harm. Clean with water and wipe with a piece of cloth so as to retain its shiny look and to avoid getting corroded.
