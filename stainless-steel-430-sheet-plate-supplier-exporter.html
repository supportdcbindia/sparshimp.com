Stainless Steel 430 Sheet and Plates

ASTM A240 / UNS S43000 / DIN 1.4016 Sheet and Plates

Stainless steel 430 sheets are thin, flat pieces of stainless steel typically used in construction, decorative, and industrial applications. They are made from alloy 430, ferritic stainless steel containing between 16 and 18% chromium and up to 0.12% carbon. This 430 magnetic stainless steel sheet is known for its excellent corrosion resistance and good formability, making it a popular choice for many applications. Some common uses for stainless steel 430 sheets include kitchen appliances, automotive parts, and construction materials.

The Stainless Steel 430 Sheets come with ASTM A240/ ASME SA240 standards. The length of SS sheets/plates comes in 2000mm/2440mm/2500mm/ 3000mm/ 6000mm, with thicknesses ranging from 0.3mm to 6mm. These sheet plates are supplied with various surface coatings such as 2B/BA/MIRROR/HR, hot rolled, cold rolled, and SATIN (met with plastic coating) Finish. It comes in various forms, such as shim sheets, perforated sheets, chequered plates, and others. These wholesale 430 stainless steel sheets/ plates are hardened by heat treatment as per ASME A and NACE MR175.

What are the benefits of 430 magnetic stainless steel sheet?

430 magnetic stainless steel sheets offer a number of impressive benefits and are becoming increasingly popular among architects and builders. Not only do 430 magnetic stainless steel sheets provide superior corrosion, stain, and rust resistance--even in extreme environments--but their high chromium content also ensures their aesthetic appeal isn't compromised by the loss of lustre over time. And since 430 magnetic stainless steel can be cut easily with regular tools, you don't have to worry about investing in expensive machinery for the job. Plus, 430 stainless steel has enough magnetism to allow it to be effective at trapping small particles like grease or dust--something that can be incredibly useful in warehouse and factory settings. All told, 430 magnetic stainless steel sheets' combination of resilient durability, affordable price point, and ease of use make them an excellent choice for professionals and hobbyists alike.

Is stainless steel 430 sheet plates good for food contact applications?

Stainless steel 430 sheet plates are ideal materials for food contact applications due to their durability, corrosion resistance, and low maintenance. It is a cost-effective solution as it can last a long time and doesn't require frequent replacement. Its nonporous surface also prevents the absorption of contaminants from the environment and bacteria from forming on it. Additionally, stainless steel 430 is easy to clean to conform with any food safety laws or regulations. All these features make stainless steel 430 an excellent choice for various food contact applications, making it a popular option for restaurants, food processing environments, and other commercial outlets.

How does stainless steel 430 sheet plates compare to other stainless steel grades, such as 304 or 316 sheet plates ?

Stainless steel 430 is a commonly used stainless steel composed of chromium and iron alloys, providing good corrosion resistance, durability, and strength. It is less expensive than grade 304 or 316 but still boasts similar ductility, making it an attractive choice for those seeking cost savings without sacrificing performance. Unlike grades 304 and 316, which are considered austenitic steels with superior corrosion resistance in many environments, grade 430 is a ferritic steel that has poor corrosion resistance in salt-rich conditions and warm climates. Though it performs lower than its austenitic counterparts in aggressive environments or under exposure to high temperatures, grade 430 offers useful qualities for the price that make it ideal for outdoor furnishings such as exposed fasteners or decorative features like trim fixtures.
