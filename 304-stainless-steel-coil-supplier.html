How Thick is 304 Stainless Steel Coil?
304 stainless steel coil thickness usually varies from 0.2mm to 6mm depending on the type and the use that will be put on the material. Standard thicknesses include 0. 5mm, 1mm and 2mm. For any other thickness, you can contact us.

Is Stainless Steel 304 Coil Waterproof?
Stainless steel 304 has high water resistance, making it ideal for many applications exposed to moisture. But while it is very resistant, it is not completely immune to water damage or rust if exposed to severe weather conditions. Maintaining the structure properly is advised so that it lasts.

Can Stainless Steel 304 Coil Be Welded?
Yes, stainless steel 304 coil can be welded, but it is recommended that it be done using appropriate methods. It is easy to weld using standard welding methods such as TIG and MIG welding, among others. To ensure that the joints maintain their strength, following certain welding practices and heat treatments after welding them is advisable.
