Which is Better, Ss 304 Plate or Ss 441 Plate?
SS 304 is more suitable for applications where moderate to high corrosion and good formability are desired. At the same time, SS 441 is more suitable for high-temperature applications where good oxidation resistance is desired. SS 304 is non-magnetic and is more versatile than SS 441, which is magnetic and more suitable for certain industries.

What Factors Affect the Pricing of Stainless Steel 441 Plates?
The cost of Stainless Steel 441 plates depends on the material's thickness, the plate's size, the current market trend, and the cost of raw materials. Further operations like cutting or finishing and variations in the price of alloying elements like chromium also affect the price.
