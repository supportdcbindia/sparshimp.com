Stainless Steel 316L Plate Supplier

SS 316L Plate, 316l Stainless Steel Plate, 316L Stainless Steel Plate Price, 316L Stainless Steel Plate Manufacturers, SS 316l Plate Supplier in India.

Sparsh Impex is one of the leading 316L stainless steel plate suppliers in Mumbai, providing SS 316L plates for various applications. Our stainless steel 316L plate is highly resistant to corrosion, particularly to chloride environments; therefore, it is ideal for marine use and chemical processing industries. The carbon content of SS 316L plates is very low, making the material very immune to sensitisation and grain boundary carbide precipitation during welding, which is crucial in enhancing durability and strength.

The 316L stainless steel plate we offer also possesses high tensile strength and good formability for fabrication and structural uses. It is used in petrochemicals, oil & gas, food processing, pharmaceutical & medical devices industries where material cleanliness & non-reactivity is paramount. In addition, stainless steel 316L plates are used to fabricate heat exchangers, condensers, pressure vessels, and tanks owing to their excellent ability to resist pitting and crevice corrosion.

Which is better ss 316 plate or ss 316L plate?
SS 316L plates provide enhanced characteristics compared to SS 316 plates because of the reduced carbon percentage. This makes SS 316L preferable in high-temperature and welded structure applications. For better strength and performance in tough conditions, SS 316L is the most preferable.
