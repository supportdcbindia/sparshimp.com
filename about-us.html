About Us

Established in 2003 with 21 years of experience, Sparsh Impex is counted as one of the privileged manufacturers, suppliers, exporters, and stockists of an admirable range of industrial products in Mumbai, India and worldwide. We offer sheets, plates, coils, angles, channels, flat bars, round bars, pipes, tubes and more in stainless steel, duplex steel, super duplex steel, Inconel, copper, cupro-nickel and other products. These products are manufactured keeping in mind the National and International Quality Standards for a wide range of applications in various Industries, including the Food Processing Industry, the Chemical Industry, the Sugar Industry, the paper and & Pulp Industry, etc.

Our Aim

Our main goal is to establish long-lasting relationships with our clients. To achieve our aim, we have committed ourselves to offer the kind of one-to-one service. So whatever your requirements, we are just a phone call or an e-mail away.

Our Team

We are supported by a team of qualified and technical professionals, who are well aware of the different techniques required to manufacture products. Our team members hold a high industry experience, which helps them in sourcing quality raw material from reputed vendors.
