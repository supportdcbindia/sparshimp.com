JT Grade Stainless Steel Sheet Plate

SS 202/JT Grade Sheets is ferritic stainless steel with low carbon content. The steel offers strong corrosion resistance in mildly corrosive settings and good oxidation resistance at high temperatures. Grade SS 202 JT Sheet is a commonly used precipitation hardening grade with excellent corrosion resistance, toughness, high hardness, and strength.

Type 202 is a less expensive alternative to traditional Cr-Ni stainless steels like 301 and 304. Manganese and nitrogen are used to substitute nickel. It is not thermally hardenable, although it can be cold-treated to high tensile strength. When annealed, Type 201 is largely nonmagnetic but becomes magnetic when cold worked. In many applications, Type 201 can be used in place of Type 301.
