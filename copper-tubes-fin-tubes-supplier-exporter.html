Sparsh Impex is a leading manufacturer, supplier, exporter and stockist of Copper Tubes and Copper Fin Tubes in Mumbai, India. We manufacture these Copper Tubes using high-quality raw material and latest technology in accordance with national and international quality standards. Our Copper Fin Tube increases the surface area of the tube compared to plain copper tubes and enhances the efficiency of heat transfer.

In general, copper fin tubes are used for different heat exchanger applications, i.e. cooling and heating of liquid and gas, including water, oil, steam and air as well as for condensation, evaporation, Chillers, Economizers, Oil Coolers, etc. Copper Fin Tubes are used widely for condensers and evaporators in sugar refineries. Our Copper Fin Tubes are well-known for their various features like low maintenance, superior corrosion resistance, high efficiency, uniform temperature distribution even for highly viscous fluids.
