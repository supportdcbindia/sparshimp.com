Quality Policy

At Sparsh Impex, our employees adhere to and contribute to the efficiency of our quality system in each aspect of our business. Our commitment guarantees to supply our clients with uncompromising quality and service. This is often achieved through a team approach where all the members are responsible towards the company objectives and work within their own discipline to make an effective contribution. To identify that the product supplied to our clients is exactly as client’s specifications, our quality control team combine technical expertise, knowledge of industry standards and the latest inspection tools and machines to satisfy all requirements. Specific attention is paid to high quality, tolerance and traceability.

Our quality assurance program maintains the highest level of quality control checks and actively contributes towards establishing and achieving the company objectives.

The quality assurance system is guided by principles that support our distinctive working culture which includes respect, self-management, open communication, and creativeness. We have adopted stringent internal control measures to make sure superior quality and zero defect products. Quality is checked at different stages of production and supply and the raw material we use is sourced solely from reliable and established vendors.
