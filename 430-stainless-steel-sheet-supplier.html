Sparsh Impex is a prominent 430 stainless steel sheet stockist based in Mumbai. SS 430 sheets are well known for their superior quality and have wide uses in several industrial fields.

430 stainless steel sheets are produced with a 16-18% chromium content. This composition enhances the high anti-oxidation and anti-corrosion characteristics of this composition. The sheets have very good formability, making them suitable for use in areas where shaping and bending is required. The 430 stainless steel sheets have a polished surface that gives them a better appearance and is easy to clean.

Our stainless steel 430 sheet is very useful and can be used in a variety of applications. Some of the application areas include automobiles, especially in the manufacture of exhaust systems, kitchen appliances and equipment. Other uses are in mullions, door and window frames, exterior cladding, and other industrial parts. Because of its long lasting and non-corrosive nature, it is suitable for indoor and outdoor use.

Can 430 stainless steel sheet be used in high-temperature?

SS 430 stainless steel is not suitable for high-temperature usage. It can withstand temperatures up to around 800°C (1472°F), but its mechanical properties and corrosion resistance may be affected at these temperatures.
