Sparsh Impex is one of the biggest suppliers of stainless steel 304 plates in Mumbai, providing high quality plates for use in different sectors. ASTM A240 Type 304 provides reliable and uniform performance in all applications. 304 stainless steel plates have high tensile strength and do not rust or oxidise, which makes them suitable for use in areas exposed to chemicals and moisture. These plates are also applied in manufacturing equipment for food processing, brewing, chemical, and marine industries where issues of hygiene and durability are paramount.
