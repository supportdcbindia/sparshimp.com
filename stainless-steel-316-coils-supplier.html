SS 316 coil has very high pitting, crevice corrosion and oxidation resistance levels in both low and high temperatures. This makes it particularly suitable for use in marine, chemical and food processing industries where exposure to chemicals and saline conditions is apparent. What is Stainless Steel Coil HS Code?
The HS Code for stainless steel coil is 7219 or 7220 in most cases, depending on the thickness and form of the coil. This code is used in customs and for trade.
