Is 316l Stainless Steel Angle Waterproof?
Although 316L stainless steel angle is immune to corrosion and can resist moisture, it is not completely “waterproof.” The material is highly resistant to rust and corrosion in wet conditions and is ideal for use in areas where water may be problematic.
