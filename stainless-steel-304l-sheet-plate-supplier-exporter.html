Stainless Steel 304L Sheet and Plates

ASTM A240 / UNS S30403 / DIN 1.4306 - 1.4307 Sheet and Plates

The SS sheets and plates of grade 304L come in ASTM A240/ ASME SA240 standard. The thickness of SS Plates /Sheets is ranging from 3mm to 20mm. It comes in various finishes such as hot rolled, cold rolled and others. These are supplied in various forms such as plain sheet, shim sheet, perforated sheet, chequered plate etc. It comes in another equivalent standard such as UNS s30403, JIS 304L, BS 304S11 and others. Before dispatch the end product to the client, it is subjected to various tests to ensure its quality.

The stainless steel of grade 304L offers many outstanding features like high strength, high durability, and long service life. These sheets and plates products of SS are made by top quality raw material and are supply all across the globe. The SS 304L plates and sheets are designed to meet the specific requirements of the customers. These SS Products are manufactured especially for high corrosive and high heat resistance applications.
