Sparsh Impex is a leading manufacturer, supplier, exporter and stockist of Admiralty brass tubes and fin tubes in Mumbai, India. We manufacture these Admiralty brass tubes using superior quality raw materials and latest technology under the guidance of our professional team. We offer multiple fin styles, all with different specifications and operative requirements in various sizes as per the requirements of our clients at the most reasonable prices. All these types of Fin Tubes are sturdy and resistant to mechanical stress and can be easily cleaned with water or steam.

Our Admiralty brass tubes are well known for their rugged construction, durable finish standards, accurate dimensions and corrosion resistance. Admiralty brass tubes have superior resistance to corrosion and is specially used for tubing of steam condensers cooled with fresh, salt or brackish water. Admiralty Brass Fin Tubes increase heat transfer rates, which not only reduces the number of tubes needed but may also reduce equipment size and cost.
