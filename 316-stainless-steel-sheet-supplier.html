What is stainless steel 316 sheet used for?
Stainless Steel 316 sheets are widely utilised in marine, chemical and pharmaceutical industries due to their features. They are best suited for use where high strength and durability are desired in extreme conditions.

Is Stainless Steel 316 Sheet suitable for welding?
Yes, Stainless Steel 316 sheets are weldable. They are easy to weld and can be welded using conventional fusion and resistance welding processes without any danger of losing strength.

What are the benefits of 316 stainless steel sheets?
Some of the benefits of 316 stainless steel sheets include Corrosion resistance, especially in areas such as marine and chemical industry, high temperature and oxidation resistance, and high durability. Chloride solutions also exhibit good weldability, formability, pitting, etc.
