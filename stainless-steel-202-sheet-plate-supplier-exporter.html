ASTM A240 / UNS S20200 / 1.4373 Sheet and Plates

Grade 202 is nickel-chromium-molybdenum-based stainless steel with good toughness at low temperatures. It is commonly utilized in precipitation hardening grades and has a variety of good and wonderful characteristics. Stainless steel sheets have properties such as high tensile strength, toughness, corrosion resistance, and durability. All of these characteristics make stainless steel 202 sheets more desirable for a variety of industrial applications.
