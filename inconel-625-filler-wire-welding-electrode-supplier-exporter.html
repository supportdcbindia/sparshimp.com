What are the applications of the inconel 625 filler rod?

Inconel 625 filler rod is an ideal product for joining metals and alloys with superior mechanical properties. It has excellent resistance to pitting, crevice corrosion and highly corrosive conditions. In addition to this, inconel 625 filler rod has good weldability and offers ease of fabrication when used in combination with inconel 625 machining processes. Furthermore, it provides good ductility even at sub-zero temperatures and produces strong joints that remain intact even at elevated temperatures. These characteristics make inconel 625 filler rod highly desirable for applications in various industries including oil & gas production, aerospace engineering, marine engineering and nuclear power systems.
