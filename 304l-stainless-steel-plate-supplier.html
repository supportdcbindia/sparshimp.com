304L Stainless Steel Plate Supplier

Sparsh Impex are delighted to offer the best and superior quality stainless steel 304L plates in Mumbai, India. High-quality SS 304L plates are manufactured for optimum service and reliability in numerous industries.

304l Stainless steel plates are well known for their high corrosion resistance, especially in acidic and alkaline media. These plates have low carbon content, reduce carbide precipitation during welding, and are suitable for high-temperature applications. They have excellent weldability and formability and their surface is smooth and have a good appearance.

304L SS plates can be used in many industries and applications due to their flexibility. They are widely applied in creating architectural façade, kitchen equipment, and chemical processing vessels. The plates are also used in industries that require the manufacturing of marine products, automotive parts, and storage tanks where the material has to withstand severe environments.

In which industries is Stainless Steel 304L Plate commonly used?
Stainless Steel 304L Plate is used in food processing, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries because of its high corrosion resistance and ease of cleaning. It is also applied for architectural purposes and structural purposes in different settings.

What are the available thicknesses of Stainless Steel 304L plate?
Stainless Steel 304L Plates are available in different thicknesses and can be in as thin as 1/16 inch (1. 6 mm) and can be as thick as 4 inches (100 mm). Other dimensions of the product can also be ordered based on thickness preferences of the client.

How is Stainless Steel 304L Plate different from Stainless Steel 304 Plate?
Stainless Steel 304L Plate is similar to Stainless Steel 304 Plate in its composition but with lower carbon content. 304L has a lower carbon content (up to 0. 03% max) than 304 (up to 0. 08% max) and is more suitable for the areas that may cause intergranular attack after welding and has better formability.
