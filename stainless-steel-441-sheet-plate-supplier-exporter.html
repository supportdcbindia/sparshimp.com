Stainless Steel 441 Sheet and Plates

ASTM A240 / UNS S44100 / DIN 1.4509 Sheet and Plates

Stainless Steel 441 Sheets/Plates carry the property of the dual stabilized Ferritic steel. In its chemical composition, the use of chromium 18% on the other side the use of the carbon is also low. SS 441 grade of steel has high-temperature strength, it’s an upgraded version of the 409-grade steel due to this it also has the property of high corrosion resistance property.

This product also has the property of good oxidation resistance that even works well in the elevated temperature. The addition of the columbium in its composition improves its longtime creep and rupture strength. This makes it suitable to be used in the automotive and tube industries.

Stainless Steel 441 Sheets/Plates, it is dual stabilized because of the titanium and niobium that provide the product excellent weld ductility when it gets a heat affected zone.

It is available in different finish form one of them is FINISH-MATT/MATTPVC/2B. Its thickness also varies according to the purposes and industries.
