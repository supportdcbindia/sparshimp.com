What Size is SS 316 Channel?
Standard sizes of SS 316 channels include depths of 1 inch to 12 inches, while the Flange width ranges from 1 inch to 6 inches. Thickness varies from 0. 1 to 0. 5 inches. Additional sizes can also be requested depending on the specific project that needs to be done.

Can Stainless Steel 316 Channel Be Welded?
Yes, Stainless Steel 316 channel is weldable. It is very suitable for welding with the right methods and filler materials to avoid corrosion and retain strength. Low-carbon versions such as 316L can be employed to reduce the chances of weld decay.
